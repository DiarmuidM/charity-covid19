                <h3 class="pcg-contrast__color-alt">Public benefits</h3>
                    <p>
        To relieve sickness and injury

Our volunteers provide First Aid &amp; Ambulance Services to members of the public across Northern Ireland. Using their skills our volunteers provide emergency care in the event of illness or injury and transport any individual(s) to a place of definitive care. Our volunteers also provide support services to the
    </p>
        <p class="pcg-charity-details__continue collapse" id="charityBenifits">
             Northern Ireland Ambulance Service and other statutory agencies by providing volunteers and Ambulances to support their Patient Transport Service. 

Advance Education &amp; Training to the Public Across Northern Ireland

Event Medical Services Northern Ireland aims to promote First Aid knowledge and training to individuals and groups across Northern Ireland by delivering various First Aid related courses. By doing so we aim to increase public awareness, knowledge and skills in the hope that should they ever be faced with a situation where their skills and knowledge are required, they can render assistance confidently using their knowledge and skills and ultimately saving a life. We would hope by training individuals in First Aid or Ambulance Aid that this may encourage them to volunteer their services through our organisation, passing on their skills and knowledge to others or by providing emergency Care at Events that our organisation works at.
