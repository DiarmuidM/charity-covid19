                <h3 class="pcg-contrast__color-alt">Public benefits</h3>
                    <p>
        The benefit that flows from the purpose is the promotion of enjoyment, better understanding and general advancement of the arts and sciences, in particular the art and science of dance, movement and theatre, for the benefit of the general public in Northern Ireland, and further abroad through touring, through exposure to and participation in, as
    </p>
        <p class="pcg-charity-details__continue collapse" id="charityBenifits">
             audience member or physical participant, the art and science of dance, movement and theatre.

These benefits are evidenced through regular internal and external qualitative assessment; regular collation of quantitative data on charitable activities, audiences, and participation; photo and video documentation; regular reporting of charitable activities and financial performance in annual reports, audited annual accounts, and reports to grant funders.

The possibility of harm, for example by physical injury incurred by participation in dance, movement and theatre, is outweighed by the benefit and significantly reduced by the policies and procedures in place to address such risks, for example Safeguarding and Child Protection Policy, Health and Safety Policy, Code of Conduct for Staff and Volunteers.

The charity&#39;s beneficiaries are the general public in Northern Ireland, and occasionally outside Northern Ireland (e.g. touring dance theatre production). Specific projects may address the needs of specific sections of society (e.g. Access to Dance Education project targeting social need in Derry City Council wards).
        </p>

                <h3 class="pcg-contrast__color-alt">What your organisation does</h3>
                    <p>
        Echo Echo Dance Theatre Company exists to produce professional movement based performance and to promote interest, participation and education in dance and movement at all levels. It bases its practice on a wide range and experience of movement influences and on the understanding that creativity and poetic sensibility in movement is a universal
    </p>
        <p class="pcg-charity-details__continue collapse" id="charityActivities">
             human attribute.

The company achieves its mission through the creation and public presentation of dance, theatre and movement performances, and by the provision of dance, theatre and movement education, and by contributing to the artistic and cultural development of general public in Northern Ireland.
