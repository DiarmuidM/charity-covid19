            <div class="pcg-charity-details__block pcg-charity-details__block--collapsible col-12">
                <h3 class="pcg-contrast__color-alt">Public benefits</h3>
                    <p>
        The direct benefits which flow from these purposes include improved health and well-being and
reduced levels of isolation. These benefits are evidenced through bi-annual evaluations carried
out with service users and general day to day feedback from beneficiaries, their families and/or
carers. These purposes do not lead to harm. The beneficiaries
    </p>
        <p class="pcg-charity-details__continue collapse" id="charityBenifits">
             of these purposes are adults with disabilities, their families and/or carers living in Northern Ireland. Currently, Jigsaw NI only
have services in three council areas - Belfast, North Down and Ards. A private benefit flowing
from the purposes is that people will be employed and get paid and staff and volunteers will receive
relevant training, which will allow them to gain skills and experiences which are transferable to
other settings. These benefits are incidental and necessary to ensure that benefit is provided to
our beneficiaries.
        </p>

            <div class="pcg-charity-details__block pcg-charity-details__block--collapsible col-12">
                <h3 class="pcg-contrast__color-alt">What your organisation does</h3>
                    <p>
        Jigsaw NI encourages adults with disabilities to participate in activities and learn new skills in a fun and supportive environment. We facilitate and run weekly term-time activities in local community venues, which reduces levels of isolation, as users are getting out of their homes and into the community. Activities include art (various forms),
    </p>
        <p class="pcg-charity-details__continue collapse" id="charityActivities">
             swimming, chair-based activity, exercise in water, boccia and keep-fit. Qualified instructors lead the activities and are supported by volunteers. Jigsaw NI organise and heavily subsidise transport where needed, as this is one of the main barriers to participation for the beneficiaries.
        </p>

            <h3 class="pcg-contrast__color-alt">Charitable purposes</h3>
            <p>The Company’s objects are specifically restricted to the preservation and protection of the health of persons with disabilities within Northern Ireland and to provide support to relieve their condition and in particular:
a) to provide facilities for recreation and other leisure time occupations in the interests of social welfare and with the object of improving the condition of life of said beneficiaries
b) to provide therapy and support to disabled persons and their families and carers and to foster an atmosphere of mutual support and encouragement among the beneficiaries their families and carers</p>
